=== GAME PROJECTS ===
🎮 Pixel Smash Bros
A 2D pixel art PVP fighting game similar to Super Smash Bros. Features local multiplayer, sound effects, health bars, and multiple maps.
  • 2-player local multiplayer
  • Custom usernames above characters
  • Sound effects (jump, attack, hit, win)
  • Health bars with real-time updates
  • 3 random maps: City, Forest, Volcano
  • Rainbow animation effects
  • Attack system with knockback
Controls:
P1: WASD (move), G (jump), F (attack)
P2: Arrows (move), K (jump), L (attack)
